  1. The Mission: Guide your white rocket to the green Goal ring.
  2. Launch: Click and drag your mouse backwards from the rocket (like pulling back a slingshot rubber band). A red dashed aiming line will appear. Release to launch!
  3. Gravity: Planets (Blue/Orange) and Black Holes will pull your rocket toward them. Use their gravity to bend your trajectory!
  4. Hazards: Do not crash into the planets, and don't fly off the screen into deep space.
  5. Sandbox Mode: Click the "Sandbox" button to build your own solar system. Click anywhere on the screen to place new planets or move the goal!
